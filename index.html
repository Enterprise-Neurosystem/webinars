Enterprise Neurosystem Webinar Series

The ENG Webinar series is a set of talks held at an interval of 2 weeks. The speakers are technical experts who discuss various issues related to use of AI in distributed environments.
Each talk is recorded and posted to the Enterprise Neurosystem Group youtube channel.


The webinars are open to the public and scheduled from 12noon-1PM Eastern on the dates mentioned in the table below.
Any one can join the webinar using the web-meeting at https://ibm.webex.com/meet/dverma.


If you would like to nominate a speaker for the webinar at one of the open slots, please send a message to the organizer through this linkedin page.
